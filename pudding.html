Fun Facts About Christmas Pudding

  • Also called "Plum Pudding": Despite the name, it contains no plums! In older recipes, "plum" referred to raisins or dried fruits.
  • Not for vegetarians!: Originally Christmas puddings contained actual minced meat, not sweet meat!
  • A Symbol of Luck: The tradition of placing a silver coin inside the pudding is said to bring wealth and luck to the finder.
  • Figgy Pudding: Mentioned in the carol “We Wish You a Merry Christmas,” Figgy pudding is similar to Christmas pudding, but includes figs as a key ingredient.
  • World Record: The largest Christmas pudding ever made weighed over 3 tonnes! It was made in Lancashire in 1992.
