What does the Pendulum Test measure?
A) How hard you can strike glass before it shatters
B) How slippery a surface is when wet
C) Contrast between surfaces
D) Reflectiveness of glazed tiles

**Answer: B) How slippery a surface is when wet**
The Pendulum Test measures the coefficient of friction, indicating how slippery a surface might be, especially under wet conditions.

Which of the following factors does NOT directly affect slip resistance?
A) Surface texture
B) Footwear material
C) Air temperature
D) Contaminants like water or oil

**Answer: C) Air temperature**
While air temperature may indirectly affect slipperiness by influencing freezing or evaporation, it doesn't directly impact the surface's slip resistance.

Which of these is the best choice for slip resistance in wet conditions?
A) Polished marble
B) Textured ceramic tiles
C) Vinyl flooring
D) Carpet

**Answer: B) Textured ceramic tiles**
Textured ceramic tiles are specifically designed for wet environments, offering superior grip, durability, and drainage without retaining water.
